Our Story

Founded in Ghana with a passion for traditional West African flavours, Ghanish brings you premium smoke-dried shrimps and herrings prepared using age-old techniques. We partner with local fishermen to source the freshest catch and smoke-dry it in hygienic, vacuum-sealed packaging, ensuring authentic taste and a shelf life of up to 12 months.

From Accra’s coast to kitchens around the globe, our mission is to share Ghanaian culinary heritage—one delicious pack at a time.

Our Mission

To deliver 100% natural, preservative-free seafood products that honour tradition and bring people together through flavour.

Our Vision

To be the world’s most trusted purveyor of West African smoke-dried seafood—celebrating culture, quality, and community.
